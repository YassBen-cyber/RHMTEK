Sensor & Hardware Specifications

Sensor Specifications

  • Target Parameters: CO, SO₂, NO₂, O₃, PM2.5, PM10, temperature, humidity, wind, solar radiation, and rainfall
  • Response & resume time: ≤ 120 seconds
  • Resolution: 1 µg/m³
  • Sensor lifespan: 3 years
  • Operating Conditions: -20°C to 50°C and 15-90% RH
  • Storage temperature: -20°C to 50°C

Device Connectivity

  • Supports LoRaWAN, Wi-Fi, LTE, and NB-IoT
  • Remote device access and configuration
  • Real-time data transmission to cloud server
  • Secure and encrypted communication protocols
  • Integrated GPS/GNSS for high-accuracy mobile monitoring

Power & Efficiency

  • Internal battery powered for 3 years
  • Power consumption ≤ 1W
  • Solar power (Optional)

Environmental Durability

  • Operating temperature: -20°C to 50°C
  • Humidity: 15-90% RH (non-condensing)
  • Barometric pressure: 81-121 kPa
  • IP65 rating

Device Dimensions & Build

  • Enclosure material: UV-resistant ABS plastic
  • Dimensions: 220 × 180 × 80 mm
  • Weight: Approximately 1.5 kg
  • Mounting options: Compatible with wall and pole mounting brackets
  • Ingress protection: weatherproof for outdoor installation
